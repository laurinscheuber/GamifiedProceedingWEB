Student Projects in undergraduate Computer Sciences

Student Projects IP34
Completion 2025 part-time

All these student projects have been realised with business partners as customers.

The projects have a duration of two semesters. The students are in their second year of study and eager to have real project experience. The student teams consist of 5-8 people which results in an overall project size of about 1800h. The projects are conducted iteratively in an agile manner with regular customer interaction. Thus customers are important to bring in their specific domain knowledge and to help shaping the product by prioritising and focussing on value generation.

The teams are coached by specialist teaching staff of the FHNW. Moreover, if more specialist knowledge is needed, students may contact any experts within the institutes.
